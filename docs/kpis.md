# KPI Definitions

### 1. Average Rating
The overall average rating submitted by customers.

### 2. Cabin Staff Service
Measures customer satisfaction with cabin crew. 

### 3. Entertainment
Rating of onboard entertainment offerings.

### 4. Food & Beverages
Calculated from customer satisfaction with meal quality and beverage service.

### 5. Ground Service
Reflects check-in, boarding, and ground crew interactions.

### 6. Seat Comfort
A comfort rating based on seat quality, legroom, and overall seating experience.

### 7. Value for Money
Customer perception of whether the flight experience matches the pice they paid.
