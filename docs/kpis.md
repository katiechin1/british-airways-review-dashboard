# KPIs — British Airways Customer Review Analysis

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the KPIs used to evaluate British Airways' customer satisfaction across aircraft types, regions, traveler segments, and time periods. These metrics provide an executive-level view of performance patterns and areas for improvement.

### Key themes revealed by the KPIs:
- Strong satisfaction on long-haul, wide-body aircraft  
- Lower ratings on short-haul and narrow-body fleets  
- Consistent service gaps in entertainment and food  
- Clear geographic, seasonal, and traveler-type differences  
- Significant rating variability across aircraft models  


### 1. Overall Customer Satisfaction KPIs
- **Average Overall Rating**  
  Measures passenger satisfaction across all reviews. Used for trend analysis and scenario evaluation.

- **Overall Rating by Month**  
  Identifies seasonal patterns, disruptions (e.g., COVID), and long-term performance shifts.

- **Overall Rating by Country**  
  Highlights geographic differences in customer ratings; the UK provides the highest-volume dataset.

### 2. Aircraft Performance KPIs
- **Average Rating by Aircraft Model**  
  Shows how customer satisfaction differs across aircraft types. Larger long-haul aircraft generally receive higher ratings.

- **Aircraft Group Comparison (Airbus vs. Boeing)**  
  Compares overall customer sentiment between the two major aircraft groups to highlight performance differences.

- **Rating Variability Across Aircraft**  
  Measures how much ratings differ between the highest and lowest rated aircraft.

### 3. Service Category KPIs
- **Cabin Staff Service Rating**  
  One of the strongest areas, showing how passengers feel about staff friendliness and service.

- **Seat Comfort Rating**  
  Reflects how comfortable passengers feel in their seats; varies a lot by aircraft and cabin class.

- **Food & Beverage Rating**  
  A consistently lower-rated area, indicating room for improvement in meals and drinks.

- **Entertainment Rating**  
  Frequently one of the weakest categories, especially on aircraft with limited in-flight options.

- **Ground Service Rating**  
  Reflects passenger experience during check-in, boarding, and airport interactions.

### 4. Traveler & Seat Type KPIs
- **Rating by Traveler Type**  
  Shows how satisfaction differs across Business, Couple Leisure, Family Leisure, and Solo travelers.

- **Rating by Seat Class**  
  Economy and First Class show similar average ratings, while Business and Premium Economy tend to score slightly lower.

### 5. Geographic KPIs
- **Rating by Continent**  
  Shows how satisfaction differs across major regions based on travel patterns and route types.

- **Country-Level Ratings**  
  Provides location-specific insights. For example, the UK has the largest review volume and shows strong preferences for certain aircraft.

### 6. Time-Based KPIs
- **Review Volume by Period**  
  Indicates how many reviews were submitted during different time frames. Volume drops sharply during COVID due to reduced travel.

- **Pandemic Period Satisfaction (2020–2021)**  
  Shows higher average ratings during this period, though based on very low review counts.

- **Year-in-Review KPI (e.g., 2022)**  
  Highlights month-to-month rating changes, including mid-year declines and late year recovery trends.
